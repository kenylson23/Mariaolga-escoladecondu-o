# Design Guidelines - Escola de Condução Maria Olga

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from modern service websites like driving schools and educational platforms, with emphasis on trust-building and professionalism while maintaining visual appeal.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Purple: 280 65% 45% (main brand color)
- Orange/Yellow: 35 85% 60% (accent and CTAs)
- Dark Blue: 220 40% 25% (text and secondary elements)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Light Purple: 280 30% 85% (backgrounds)
- Cream: 45 50% 95% (neutral backgrounds)
- White: 0 0% 100% (primary background)

### B. Typography
**Primary Font**: Inter or Poppins (Google Fonts)
- Headings: 600-700 weight
- Body text: 400-500 weight
- Accent text: 500-600 weight

**Hierarchy:**
- H1: 3xl-4xl, bold
- H2: 2xl-3xl, semibold
- H3: xl-2xl, medium
- Body: base-lg, regular

### C. Layout System
**Spacing Units**: Tailwind 4, 8, 12, 16, 24 units
- Sections: py-16 to py-24
- Cards: p-6 to p-8
- Elements: gap-4 to gap-8

### D. Component Library

**Navigation:**
- Clean header with logo and horizontal menu
- Purple background with white text
- Mobile hamburger menu

**Hero Section:**
- Large hero image of driving instruction
- Overlay with purple gradient (280 65% 45% to transparent)
- White text with orange CTA button
- Height: 70-80vh

**Course Cards:**
- White background with subtle shadow
- Purple accent borders
- Orange pricing badges
- Category icons (A, B, C, D license types)

**Contact Forms:**
- Purple outline inputs
- Orange submit buttons
- Dark blue labels

**Gallery:**
- Grid layout for vehicle and facility photos
- Hover effects with purple overlay

### E. Key Sections
1. **Hero**: Large image with school presentation
2. **About**: School history and differentials
3. **Courses**: License categories with pricing
4. **Gallery**: Photos of vehicles and facilities
5. **Contact**: Location, phone, hours, contact form

## Images
- **Hero Image**: Large photo of instructor with student in car or modern driving school facade
- **Course Images**: Different vehicle types (car, motorcycle, truck) for each license category
- **Gallery Images**: School interior, modern vehicles, happy students, instructors
- **About Image**: Founder Maria Olga or school building exterior

## Visual Treatment
**Gradients**: Purple to dark blue for overlays and accents
**Backgrounds**: Predominantly white with purple accent sections
**Contrast**: High contrast with dark blue text on light backgrounds
**Modern Elements**: Rounded corners (8px), subtle shadows, clean typography

This creates a trustworthy, professional appearance while maintaining visual appeal through the vibrant color scheme.
